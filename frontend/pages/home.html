About CritterConnect

Based in the heart of Singapore, CritterConnect is a community-driven initiative dedicated to helping our local wildlife thrive. Whether it’s a wounded pigeon in your neighbourhood or a stray kitten by the park, we make it easy for anyone to report injured animals and connect them with rescue organisations.

Our Guidebook helps you identify and learn about common species in Singapore — from squirrels to hornbills — so we can all coexist with our city’s amazing biodiversity.
